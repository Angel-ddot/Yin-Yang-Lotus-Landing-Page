About Us

At Yin & Yang Lotus, our mission is to help foster children find the right adoptive families, access higher education, and build the life skills they need to succeed. From providing housing to guiding them through interviews and job opportunities, we ensure every young person has the support they need to step into the world with confidence.

Our Programs

Adoption Support

We connect foster children with the right families to ensure a safe, loving, and lasting home environment.

Education Access

Providing scholarships, mentorship, and application guidance so foster youth can pursue college or vocational training.

Career Preparation

From interview coaching to resume building, we prepare youth for the workforce with confidence and professionalism.

Housing & Life Skills

Offering temporary housing, budgeting guidance, and everyday skills to help foster youth transition into independence.
